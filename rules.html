Rules

Who are we?

Corpulent is a semi-hardcore guild that was founded a few weeks into Classic launch and is built around a strong core of experienced raiders. Our main goal is to create a fun raiding environment while still getting the job done. As a raider you are expected to be on time for raids and come prepared with the required buffs and consumables.

Our raiding schedule will never exceed two mandatory raids per week and we will try to clear content as fast as possible with speedruns accuring monthly. During progress this might change slightly

To achieve this we are looking for likeminded players to fill our raids with. So if you are an ambitious player who wants to experience and clear all content that Classic has to offer while having a good time, contact an officer ingame or on discord.

What we expect from you?

- Full Prebis with enchants or as close as reasonably possible.

- Capable of receiving constructive (and unconstructive) criticism. Preferably with a sense of humour.

- PvE talent build is not a requirement during farm, but we expect you to fulfill the role you joined the guild as.

- You are active in our Discord server and are required to be on discord during you raids.

- Engineering for sappers is not a requirement, but we think it is something good that everyone should get.

- You use the consumables we require

What loot system do you use?

- Corpulent uses a Loot Council system as we strongly believe it is the most fair way to gear a raid. Loot is awarded based on performance, attendance, activity and other factors including attitude.

What do I need to know before joining your raid?

Read below before joining your first raid with Corpulent. You are expected to have read and taken all the information on the Discord server, to make it a little easier to get started, below are some details on how to get started and stay informed. You are expected to communicate and keep the guild up to date, especially with regard to raiding deviations.

Before you sign

Before you even try to sign a raid, you need to achieve a good level of gear and preparation. Preparation requires that you understand all of the raid's encounters and bossfights, you must have the correct specc with the necessary enchants on the gear and preraid BiS items. In addition, the following applies:

- All the pre-quest and attunes for all raids in the current Phase.

- Install RCLootCouncil addon (https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/rclootcouncil-classic)

- Any threatmeter addon and DeadlyBossMod addons installed and updated.

- Practical addons for the class you are playing.

Sign up for raid

To get a chance to join a raid, you must first sign up during the raid signup by clicking on the appropriate emoji for your class in. If you cannot do any of the days, you MUST click on the emojin representing an: absence: (Absence). We always assume that anyone who has the ambition to raid can attend all raid days each week unless Absence is listed.

If there is an obstacle when you are already expected to come to a raid, You use the #raid-abscense channel.

Join the raid

We have the ambition to raid as many days a week when new raid content is out for progression until farm mode, after that we will try to clear every mandatory raid in 1-2 days. Thursday and Sunday is our mainraids (Naxxramas for the moment) day were we progress new content). We invite around 1hour before raid starts with summon groups, if you are not online and ready to accept the invite before raid time starts your place in the raid is not guaranteed.

During the raid

Are you unsure of boss mechanics, who to heal, how we deal with different bosses, etc. Ask! we rather see you ask than make mistakes! contact your comrade or raid leader/officer.

Make sure you have full control of your assignments before the first pull. We do not go AFK during raids, but if you need to run away for a short time it should be done other than before bossfights and make sure one of the leaders in the raid knows. Later, as we run longer raids, AFK breaks will be announced for the entire raid by Raid Leader
