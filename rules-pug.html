How to sign up for raids?

Make sure to join the discord of Corpulent Pug Life and signup for the event you wish to attend.

Please note, sign-ups are not a first-come, first-serve basis. We base all rosters on past raid performance (via WarcraftLogs), current gear, and general player knowledge of the game.

1. Read all info in #welcome, #raid-info, and #loot-rules channels! Then change your server nicknames to match your in-game name respective to the roles you wish to be considered for: <Guild> Character i.e. <Corp> Codenamemars
2. When we release sign-ups, select the role you would like to be considered for and send screenshots of your gear and a link to your WarcraftLogs to @ Code if you haven't previously. AQ Nature Resistance (100 for melee) and Shadow Resistance (150 for warlocks) screenshots are sent to @ Code, all unbuffed. Frost wands are also required for warlocks.
3. We will lock the sign ups and create the roster in the Raid Helper bot.
